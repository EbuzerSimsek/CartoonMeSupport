🧾 Common Questions

  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    You can cancel your subscription any time from your Apple ID account settings under "Subscriptions".
  • I didn’t receive my cartoon result. What should I do?
    Please wait a few minutes and check again. If it still doesn't show, send us a message with your order details.
  • Can I use the app without a subscription?
    Some features may be limited without a subscription, but you can still try basic tools for free.

🔄 Restore Purchases

If you’ve purchased a subscription and it’s not showing up, use the “Restore Purchases” button inside the app settings.
